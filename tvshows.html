TV Shows

I enjoy thrilling and well-written TV series with strong characters and gripping plots.

I am Not a Robot

Category: Romantic Comedy, Sci-Fi

Synopsis: A romantic comedy about a man allergic to humans and a woman pretending to be a robot.

Director: Jung Dae-yoon

Stars: Yoo Seung-ho, Chae Soo-bin

Kill Me Heal Me

Category: Psychological, Drama, Romance

Synopsis: A wealthy man struggles with dissociative identity disorder and seeks the help of a psychiatrist.

Director: Kim Jin-man

Stars: Ji Sung, Hwang Jung-eum

Strong Woman Do Bong Soon

Category: Action, Romantic Comedy, Fantasy

Synopsis: A young woman born with superhuman strength gets hired as a bodyguard to a gaming company CEO and falls in love while fighting crime.

Director: Lee Hyung-min

Stars: Park Bo-young, Park Hyung-sik
